About Me

I was born in an insignificant hovel outside Senden. I was raised by the Order. A hard life for an unflinching purpose. It made me who I am. I am grateful for the trust placed in me. Some men are born to purpose. Perhaps I am such a one. My path is as straight as an archer's shot, unhindered by darkness or fear. My struggle will end only with my death, and my soul in Sigmar's care.
